### Upgrade/Downgrade Plan

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.primeflow.com/v1/billing/subscription \
  -H "X-Primeflow-Key: pfk_live_xxx" \
  -H "X-Org-Id: org_123" \
  -d '{
    "plan_id": "growth"
  }'
```

### Usage (mode-aware)

```bash
curl "https://api.primeflow.com/v1/billing/usage?mode=live" \
  -H "X-Primeflow-Key: pfk_live_xxx" \
  -H "X-Org-Id: org_123"
```

## Usage Tracking

Your usage is tracked in real-time:

- **Transactions** — Successful payments (metered per org and mode)
- **Connections** — Sandbox and live connection counts
- **Workflows** — Workflow executions (where enabled)
- **Webhooks** — Webhook delivery attempts (where enabled)

### Usage Notifications

You'll receive email alerts at:
- 80% of transaction limit
- 90% of transaction limit
- 100% of transaction limit

## Overages

For Starter and Growth plans, you can exceed your included transactions:

| Plan | Overage Fee |
|------|-------------|
| Starter | $0.05/transaction |
| Growth | $0.02/transaction |

Overages are billed at the end of each billing period.

## FAQ

### When am I billed?
Monthly, on the anniversary of your signup date.

### Can I change plans mid-cycle?
Yes. Upgrades are prorated. Downgrades take effect next billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my limit on Free?
Payments are blocked until the next billing cycle or you upgrade.

### Do failed payments count against my limit?
No, only successful payments count.

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for any plan.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, cancel anytime. No long-term contracts.
